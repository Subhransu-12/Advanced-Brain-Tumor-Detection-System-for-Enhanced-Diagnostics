You have a Stage 3 of Brain Tumor

Overview :

Grade 3 tumors are malignant and grow more rapidly than lower-grade tumors. Cells look notably abnormal under the microscope, and they are likely to infiltrate surrounding brain tissue. There is a higher chance of recurrence after treatment.

Treatment:

  • Surgery: To remove as much of the tumor as safely possible.
  • Radiation therapy: Almost always used after surgery.
  • Chemotherapy: Typically used along with radiation.
