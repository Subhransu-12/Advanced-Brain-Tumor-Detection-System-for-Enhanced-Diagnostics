You have a Stage 4 of Brain Tumor

Overview :

Grade 4 tumors are the most aggressive and fastest-growing brain tumors. Cells look very abnormal under the microscope, and they often spread quickly to surrounding brain tissue. Grade 4 tumors may contain areas of dead tissue (necrosis).

Treatment:

  • Surgery: Aims to remove as much of the tumor as safely possible.
  • Radiation therapy: Used after surgery in almost all cases.
  • Chemotherapy: Generally used along with radiation.
  • Other therapies: Targeted therapies, tumor treating fields (TTF), and clinical trials may be options depending on individual circumstances.
