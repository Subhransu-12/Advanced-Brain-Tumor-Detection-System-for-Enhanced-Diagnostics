Medical Report - Stage 2 Brain Tumor

Diagnosis:

A Grade 2 tumor has been detected. These tumors are malignant (cancerous) but slow-growing. They can spread into nearby brain tissue and may recur.

Treatment Options:

  • Surgery: To remove as much of the tumor as possible.
  • Radiation therapy: May be used after surgery to destroy remaining tumor cells and reduce the risk of recurrence.
  • Chemotherapy: Sometimes used in addition to radiation, especially if the tumor is deemed higher risk.
